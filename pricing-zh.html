常見問題
我來自一家小型初創公司，有折扣嗎？
當然。我們十分支持初創公司♥️。如果您的團隊是5人或更少，我們有特價，只為您哦。請發送電子郵件至hello@timlio.com以獲取更多信息。
您是否為非營利組織提供折扣？
We do. Just reach out to us at hello@timlio.com. We will take it from there.
What’s included in the closed beta?
Everything. Everyone starts from the closed beta so that you can try all of the features. No credit card required to start using Timlio!
How do I contact Timlio support?
Please email us at hello@timlio.com and we will get back to you as soon as possible.
How do you count the number of users?
You have you tell us. Timlio is enabled for everyone on the Slack team, except guest users by default. We will only bill you for the people for whom Timlio is enabled.
What if I add or remove employees?
If you add users or employees in the middle of your billing cycle, you’ll be charged a prorated amount for that month. When you remove employees your payment amount will be adjusted the following month.
